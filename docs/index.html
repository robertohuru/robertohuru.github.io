Land Administration · QGIS Plugin 🏛️

GLSC Supplant QGIS Plugin

The supplant tool was developed as a plugin for QGIS to help construct high-quality digital representations of Guyanan survey plans. These plans have laid out Guyana's spatial planning process that allows land development since the late 18th century.

This work was conducted in context of the FAO-GLSC Guyana Sustainable Land Development and Management (SLDM) Project that is capacitating the Guyana Lands and Surveys Commission (GLSC) and is funded by the Guyana REDD+ Investment Fund (GRIF).

📈 Major efficiency gain over previous paper-based workflows
Python QGIS API PyQGIS GDAL PostGIS
Agriculture. Statistics. ETL 🏛️

Statistics from Space System

Web-based system for visualizing agricultural crop statistics in Mozambique. This data platform shows the various original source data sets, various derived data products and the monitoring device that allows tracking fieldwork.

Crop statistics data are then aggregated to district, province or national level.

Graph. Visualization. Ontology 🏛️

The Living TextBook (LTB)

Web-based system for visualizing information in a large map of concepts and understand the relationships between them.

The Living Textbook is a novel way to display content digitally. It combines the versatility of a wiki with the visual representation of concepts and their relationships. It allows experts to create a mind map of their domain and offers learners the freedom to explore concepts through their relationships.
